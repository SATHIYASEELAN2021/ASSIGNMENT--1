SIES (Nerul) College of Arts, Science, and Commerce was established in the year 1998. The college is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC). The college has been re-accredited with Grade ‘A’ by NAAC. At present, SIES Nerul is offering 8 undergraduate degree courses and 4 postgraduate degree courses in the field of Arts, Science, and Commerce. Admission to the UG and PG courses are purely based on merit as per the Mumbai University guidelines.
